# Financial Projections

## Model Assumptions

All projections are bottom-up (user count × ARPU), not top-down (% of market). Conservative case is the planning case. Every assumption listed so investors can stress-test it.

**Key assumptions:**
- App Store launch: Month 1
- No paid acquisition until Month 10 (organic-first)
- App Store takes 30% in Year 1, 15% after first $1M (Small Business Program)
- Annual plan mix: 40% of Pro subscribers by Month 6, 60% by Month 18
- Monthly churn: 6% at launch, improves to 3.5% by Month 18 with product improvements
- Team/B2B: First contract at Month 5, 3 by Month 9, 10 by Month 18
- Average team size: 18 athletes, $4.99/user/month, billed annually

## Path to Series A

**Series A criteria (standard 2025 bar):**
- $1M ARR run rate (we hit this in Month 22 base case, Month 14 bull case)
- D30 retention >30% (our target: 35%)
- <5% monthly churn (our target: 3.5%)
- Proven CAC with paid channels (Month 10–18)
- 2+ revenue streams showing (consumer + B2B)

**Likely Series A size**: $3–6M at $18–28M pre-money valuation
**Likely investors**: a16z consumer fund, Founders Fund, Benchmark (health/consumer focus)
