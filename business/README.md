## The One-Liner

> ELOS is the first app that unifies fitness, nutrition, and academics for competitive students — giving them a single operating system for their life, not five fragmented ones.

## Why Now

- Gen Z is the most health-conscious generation ever, but also the most academically pressured
- No app connects physical performance to academic performance
- High school athletes represent a $2B+ addressable market with near-zero penetration
- AI makes personalized coaching feasible at consumer cost for the first time

## The Ask (for pitch)

**Seed round: $500K**
- 40% product & engineering
- 30% user acquisition
- 20% operations
- 10% legal/IP/corporate

**Use of funds runway: 18 months** to reach 10,000 paid users and Series A metrics.
